Evolution Of Design

  • Illustration
    llustrations are often included as part of web, marketing, and brand design.
  • Infographic
    Infographics come in the form of 2D illustrations, interactive elements on a website, or even videos.
  • UI/UX
    Designing Web/App Interface
  • Web Design
    A graphic design that is broadly used in almost every sphere of our regular activities. In other words, its also recognized as communication design in terms of the power of connecting elements to focus the message visually.
  • Publication Design
    Digital publication design utilizes traditional elements of art such as color, typography, and space.
  • Brand Design
    Graphic design is more than just aesthetic appeal. It can help businesses to reach new customers, build brand awareness and drive sales.
  • Modernism
    Early modernism was a time when artists and designers broke from tradition to create a new style of design which would influence all artists and designers.
  • Pop Art
    Pop Art. Primarily a British and American cultural phenomenon that gained traction in the late 1950s and 1960s.
  • Painting
    Painting is a visual art, which is characterized by the practice of applying paint, pigment, color or other medium to a solid surface.
